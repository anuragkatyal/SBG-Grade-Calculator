Grade Calculation:

Standards-Based Grading Scale:

  • A: 10 CORE + 4, 5, or 6 OTHER
  • B: 10 CORE + 2 or 3 OTHER
  • C:
    • 10 CORE + 1 OTHER, OR
    • 10 CORE (no others needed), OR
    • 9 CORE + 4, 5, or 6 OTHER, OR
    • 8 CORE + 5 or 6 OTHER
  • D:
    • 9 CORE + 1, 2, or 3 OTHER, OR
    • 8 CORE + 1, 2, 3, or 4 OTHER, OR
    • 8 CORE (no others needed)
  • F: 7 or fewer CORE

Final Exam Impact Rules:

  • 60% or higher: Grade can only improve or stay the same
    • A on final (90-100%) → A overall (if initial was lower)
    • B on final (80-89%) → B overall (if initial was lower)
    • C on final (70-79%) → C overall (if initial was lower)
  • 50-59%: Grade drops one letter
  • Below 50%: Grade drops two letters
