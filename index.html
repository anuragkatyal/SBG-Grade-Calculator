Standards-Based Grading Scale

  • A: 10 CORE + 4, 5, or 6 OTHER
  • B: 10 CORE + 2 or 3 OTHER
  • C:
    • 10 CORE + 1 OTHER, OR
    • 10 CORE (no others needed), OR
    • 9 CORE + 4, 5, or 6 OTHER, OR
    • 8 CORE + 5 or 6 OTHER
  • D:
    • 9 CORE + 1, 2, or 3 OTHER, OR
    • 9 CORE (no others needed), OR
    • 8 CORE + 1, 2, 3, or 4 OTHER, OR
    • 8 CORE (no others needed)
  • F: 7 or fewer CORE

Final Exam Impact Rules

  • 90% or higher: Grade improves by up to 3 letter grades
  • 80–89.99%: Grade improves by up to 2 letter grades
  • 70–79.99%: Grade improves by up to 1 letter grade
  • 60–69.99%: No change to grade
  • 50–59.99%: Grade drops by up to 1 letter grade
  • 40–49.99%: Grade drops by up to 2 letter grades
  • Below 40%: Grade drops by up to 3 letter grades
